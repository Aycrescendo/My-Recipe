Recipe for Cooked Beans

Beans are a versatile and nutritious food, rich in protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals


Recipe Information

  • Preparation Time: 20minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1hour
  • Number of Servings: 6
  • Difficulty level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of uncooked beans
  • 70ml of Palm oil
  • 2 medium sized Onions
  • 1 Seasoned cube
  • 3 table spoons of grounded-Cray fish
  • Blended dried pepper
  • Some riped plantains

Instructions

  1. Rinse the uncooked beans into a pot
  2. Add fresh water
  3. Add Onions
  4. Bring it to a boil
  5. Reduce the heat and leave for a while
  6. Add dried blended pepper
  7. Cut the plantains in it
  8. add small salt, seasoned cube and cray fish to taste
  9. Add palm oil, then allow it to steam for a while
  10. Now it's done

Notes

Feel free to adjust the spice level by adding more if you like
